Meet Our Owner

Jay Rathod

Founder & CEO of ConsoleVerse

A lifelong gamer with over 10 years of experience in the gaming industry, Jay founded ConsoleVerse in 2025 with a vision to create the ultimate destination for gaming enthusiasts.

Prior to launching ConsoleVerse, Jay worked with several major gaming companies, developing a deep understanding of what gamers truly want and need. This experience has shaped the core values of ConsoleVerse: quality, innovation, and community.

"Gaming isn't just about entertainment—it's about creating experiences that bring people together and push the boundaries of what's possible. At ConsoleVerse, we're committed to delivering those experiences to gamers everywhere."

When not working on expanding the ConsoleVerse brand, Jay can be found testing the latest games, attending industry conferences, and mentoring the next generation of gaming entrepreneurs.
